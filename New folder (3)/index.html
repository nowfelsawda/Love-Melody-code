Love:Melody

Be My Melody

Independent 7 Member Co-Ed Group

Members: Joy, Sapphire, RashKam, Violet, Chae, Medusa, Eiko

Love:Melody is a unique and diverse co-ed group of talented individuals, each bringing their own flavor to our music.

Joy

Main Vocalist, Songwriter

Oldest member, Nigerian (foreigner line)

Born: April 13, 2002

Age: 20

Joy is the main vocalist of the group and is part of our songwriters. She brings her incredible voice and creative writing skills to our melodies.

RashKam

Lead Vocalist, Subrapper, Poet

Second oldest member, Indian

Born: July 17, 2005

Age: 18

RashKam is the lead vocalist and subrapper of Love:Melody. She's also a talented poet and has released her own poetry collection book. She brings her unique style and artistic talents to our melodies.

She's the proud owner of a cute dog named Reo.

Sapphire

Lead Vocalist, Songwriter

Born: August 21, 2005

Age: 18

Sapphire is the lead vocalist of Love:Melody and is also a part of our songwriters. With her powerful voice and creative songwriting skills, she contributes to our unique melodies.

She has an adorable bunny named Doodle.

Violet

Main Vocalist, Professional Singer

Born: February 10, 2006

Age: 17

Violet is the main vocalist of Love:Melody and a professional singer. His exceptional vocal skills add a unique touch to our melodies.

He hails from India and brings his vibrant personality to the group.

Chae

Main Rapper, Dancer

Born: September 21, 2006

Age: 16

Chae is the main rapper of Love:Melody and he's also a passionate dancer. Living in the Middle East, he brings a unique cultural influence to our group.

He loves to groove to the rhythm and express himself through dance.

Medusa

Main Rapper, Coder, Music Lover

Second Youngest Member

Born: November 26, 2007

Age: 15

Medusa is the main rapper and the second youngest member of Love:Melody. She's not only skilled in rapping but also loves coding and listening to music.

She's a proud owner of 3 cats named Twinkle, Gucci, and Sacci.

Eiko

Leader, Songwriter

Youngest Member

Born: May 15, 2010

Age: 13

Eiko is the youngest member of Love:Melody and holds the position of leader and main rapper. Alongside leading the group, he's also a skilled songwriter and editor.

He's responsible for the editing and management of the group.

He has the most adorable doreo looking dog oreo
